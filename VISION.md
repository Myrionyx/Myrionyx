# Vision

## Who We Are

Myrionyx is an independent open research initiative dedicated to exploring new theoretical, mathematical, and conceptual approaches in biophysics and biological systems.

Our goal is to create an international collaborative environment where scientists, engineers, mathematicians, and students can openly exchange ideas, challenge assumptions, and develop innovative concepts.

---

## Our Mission

To develop open theoretical frameworks and future biomedical concepts through interdisciplinary collaboration.

---

## Our Values

* Open scientific discussion
* Respectful collaboration
* Curiosity before certainty
* Critical thinking
* Transparency
* Interdisciplinary research

---

## Long-Term Vision

We aim to build a global community capable of developing new mathematical frameworks, conceptual diagnostic technologies, and theoretical models inspired by biological systems.

This initiative welcomes both supportive and critical perspectives. Scientific progress depends on constructive discussion, reproducibility, and continuous refinement of ideas.
