# Research Principles

The Myrionyx initiative follows several guiding principles.

## Curiosity

Every important scientific discovery begins with a question.

## Transparency

Hypotheses should always be distinguished from validated scientific knowledge.

## Interdisciplinary Thinking

Biology, mathematics, physics, engineering, and computer science are complementary rather than isolated disciplines.

## Open Collaboration

Respectful discussion is encouraged regardless of academic background.

## Continuous Improvement

Ideas evolve through criticism, testing, and refinement.
