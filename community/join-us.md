# Join Myrionyx

## Become Part of an Open Research Initiative

Myrionyx is an independent open research initiative exploring theoretical biology, biophysics, mathematics, and conceptual biomedical technologies.

We welcome researchers, students, engineers, programmers, designers, and curious thinkers from around the world.

---

## Who Can Join?

We encourage contributions from people with backgrounds in:

- Biophysics
- Biology
- Medicine
- Mathematics
- Physics
- Biomedical Engineering
- Computer Science
- Artificial Intelligence
- Data Science
- Bioinformatics
- Electrical Engineering
- Philosophy of Science
- Scientific Visualization

---

## Ways to Contribute

You can contribute by:

- Proposing new ideas
- Improving documentation
- Suggesting mathematical formulations
- Creating conceptual device designs
- Developing simulations
- Designing figures and diagrams
- Reviewing scientific literature
- Opening discussions
- Reporting issues
- Sharing constructive criticism

---

## Current Collaboration Opportunities

We are currently looking for collaborators interested in:

- Mirror Mathematics

- Scientific Visualization
- Open Science

---

## Our Philosophy

Ideas grow through collaboration.

Every contribution—large or small—is appreciated.

Respectful scientific discussion is always encouraged.

---

## How to Start

1. Explore the repositories.
2. Read the documentation.
3. Open an Issue if you have an idea.
4. Join an existing discussion.
5. Submit a Pull Request if you would like to contribute.

We look forward to building the future of interdisciplinary research together.
